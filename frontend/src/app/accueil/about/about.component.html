Our Story

The University of Sousse aims to improve its participation in the programs of Research and Innovation funding, in this case the R&I Framework Program Horizon 2020. Within this framework, a project engineering unit is set up to strengthen the skills of the scientific and industrial community in terms of mounting of international projects. The main mission of this unit is to get around the set of difficulties encountered by potential project leaders and will ensure the implementation of a technical support process for submission.
